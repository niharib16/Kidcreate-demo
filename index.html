PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Kid Create Studio – Ashburn × Oak View Elementary School

April 14, 2026

This agreement is entered into between Kid Create Studio – Ashburn ("Provider") and Oak View Elementary School ("Partner") for the delivery of on-site art education programs for the Spring/Summer 2026 semester.

Program Sessions Schedule

ENROLLMENT — A minimum of 6 students must be enrolled for each session to run. The Partner will provide enrollment numbers no later than 48 hours before each scheduled session.

BILLING — The Partner will be invoiced at the conclusion of the program semester. Invoices are calculated per student per session attended. Payment is due within 30 days of receipt via credit card or check.

CANCELLATIONS — Either party may cancel a session with 48 hours notice. Kid Create Studio reserves the right to cancel any session that does not meet the minimum enrollment threshold.
